How it works

  • Interacts via web/mobile at the consumer level, ensuring the information, response and reassurance
  • Automates and tracks all processes necessary to convey land, reducing insurance costs to delver settlements more quickly and consistently
  • Provides data analytics for clients who are developers. Data will also be package
  • Parcels out piecework within the transaction to home-based & remote knowledge workers
  • Deconstructed process allows for the progressive automation of discrete stages over time
  • Delivers lower cost to consumer while maintaining attractive margins and also enabling ConvX to scale with more agility than the traditional employee base model
  • Integrates with other real estates platforms to create an end to end real estate
